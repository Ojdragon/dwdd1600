About us!

How'd I get here?

This website is but a small piece of a huge journey! A journey that began when I was very young! My name is Olivia and I'm from Provo, Utah A smallish town surrounded by mountains. I have always loved to draw and create. I've explored every medium from charcoal to water color, however nothing really stuck, I was never truly able to draw it how I saw it in my mind, that is until I discovered digital art! I founf my true potential as an artist in 2021 when I was gifted an art tablet. Given to me by my amazing mom Shannon. I took to it immediately and started to create more in depth and exciting artwork then ever before. Now I am a thriving artist and I was inspired to create this sight so that I can show my artowrk to the world!

This website

After growing my portfolio and realizing I wanted to sell I thought: well I need a way to sell now don't I? I just so happened to be taking a Web development class, where I was assigned the task of creating a website. Well I thought, I might as well just make it here for my assignment!
