What is Kiwi Doodles?

Kiwi Doodles is a sight focused on selling high quality digital art! I suppose i should introduce myself, my name is Olivia and I am both the artist and the creator of this website. I believe that everyone needs color in their lives and through my unique works of art I intend on giving color to everyone. My pieces are designed to be eye catching and exciting, perfect for any home in desperate need of an upgrade, and if nothing I have available catches your fancy, please send me a request or a comission and let me know what I can make for you!
